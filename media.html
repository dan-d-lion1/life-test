Leukemic Indigents Fund Endowment (L.I.F.E.) Video

L.I.F.E. Admin | Nov 19, 2011

L.I.F.E. assists leukemic children from indigent families achieve remission and cure. We call this the "Adopt a Leukemic Child Program", where each indigent leukemic child is clinically adopted by at least one benefactor who will shoulder the full cost of treatment (currently roughly P380,000) over a 3-year period. This amount will cover chemotherapy, laboratory and antibiotics.
