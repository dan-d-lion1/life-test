Activities

Quarterly L.I.F.E. Birthday Party Celebrations underscore the gift of life for those patients celebrating their birthdays within the quarter. With the participation of their families, friends, attending physicians and other medical staff, these events are sponsored by Assumption Batch‚Äù82 and held every last Saturday of the second month of each calendar quarter.

Special Birthday Celebrations for L.I.F.E. are offered by individual sponsors who wish to celebrate their own birthdays with the indigent leukemic children. It is experiencing the joy and happiness in these children that makes one appreciate and celebrate life indeed.

Field Trips for the Children of L.I.F.E. are sponsored by groups and associations such as Jaycees, Rotary Clubs. Trips to museums, carnivals and parks allow the children to experience the beauty of creation in the midst of their own suffering.

L.I.F.E Foundation Day Celebrations find the people behind L.I.F.E. coming together to thank God for the healing of those patients cured during the past 12 months, and for the benefactors who lent support to the Foundation the past year. They are held between July 13, the birthday of L.I.F.E. Founder Dr. Alendry Caviles, Jr.(+), and July 17, the anniversary of the incorporation of the Foundation.
