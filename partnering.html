Guided by its vision, L.I.F.E. exists to serve two purposes. The first is to assist leukemic children from indigent families achieve remission. The second is to provide optimum care in response to the children’s material, psychosocial, and spiritual needs

The benevolence and generosity and kindness of these friends have made it possible for us to render full or partial anti-leukemia treatment, diagnostics, and support care to hundreds of indigent leukemic children, allowing them to join the mainstream of normal life.

WHAT ARE THE MAIN CHALLENGES OF THE FUTURE?

Every year, the clinic admits about 25 new patients afflicted with leukemia. With financial support, roughly three out of four can achieve remission if treatment is sustained for 3 years. Seeking supporters who can commit to the program over this 3-year period is a continuing challenge for the Foundation.

There too is the question of support for those patients who are, from the outset, considered poor-risk patients. Their chance for survival is slim but they too need financial support to sustain them for the remaining short time that they have left. The funds of the Foundation are earmarked primarily for good-risk patients, there is a need therefore to strengthen the support to provide some comfort for poor-risk patients.

Finally, the adoption of indigent leukemic children by way of chemotherapy and diagnostics is extended only to our patients based in Luzon (the main island in the Philippines) who can be expected to keep their appointments. Patients based in the islands south of Luzon have had to contend with the high cost of travel due to poor infrastructure. Funding support to them to defray travel expenses may be raised as a start. Major related endeavors in the future may include a L.I.F.E. housing facility near the hospital, much like the McDonald's Homes located beside leukemia centers in America. Expanding L.I.F.E. to create centers in hospitals in the provincial areas too would be a high-impact program for the Foundation.

Much therefore still need to be done. We offer you to partner with L.I.F.E. in making the dream of many children come true --- the dream of a longer life, free from leukemia.
