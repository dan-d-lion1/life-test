Guided by its vision, L.I.F.E. exists to serve two purposes. The first is to assist leukemic children from indigent families achieve remission. The second is to provide optimum care in response to the children’s material, psychosocial, and spiritual needs

L.I.F.E. Vision

 

Life begets life.

We are volunteers to the cause of overcoming the challenges attendant to Leukemia.

We believe that each life serves a purpose. The healthy, the stricken. Those who have plenty and those with naught.

We believe that life is a resource from which much can be drawn, whether material or spiritual, and that it is to be shared.

Our purpose is to get lives together and to provide the opportunity for those with more in life and of life, to share with those with less.

We see a world where hope and comfort abound. We see a world where every life can serve its purpose – And its highest purpose is to suffuse life with life.

Guiding Objective

To assist leukemic children from indigent families achieve remission and to provide optimum care in response to their material, psycho-socialand spiritual needs. - S. E. C. Reg. No. A1095-02652
