Leukemic Indigents Fund Endowment (L.I.F.E.) Testimonial

L.I.F.E. is non-profit foundation duly registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). It aims to help leukemic children from impoverished families achieve remission, cure, as well as get physical, emotional, and spiritual help.
