Dear Friend,

This yuletide season, let us give love and give l.i.f.e.

Since 1995, Hallmark for L.I.F.E. has helped alleviate the burden of leukemic children from indigent families and intensified public awareness about the truth behind the dreaded disease - leukemia can be cured through continuous medical treatment.  With this inspiration, Filstar Distributors Corporation, the country's exclusive licensee of Hallmark Cards, and the Leukemic Indigents Fund Endowment (L.I.F.E.) Foundation created this charitable project.

A line of specially-crafted greeting cards, uniquely designed by talented local artists and photographers, capture the vibrancy of Christmas in the Philippines.  Send your business partners and loved ones your greetings this holiday season and make them feel special and loved.  Each Hallmark for l.i.f.e. card you send allows a leukemic child, under the wing of l.i.f.e. Foundation, enjoy a merry Christmas, too.

Now on its 18th year, your help is once again needed to spread the message and support the cause.  Let the message of Christmas be more memorable this coming season -- send a card, give l.i.f.e.

Our warm holiday wishes and sincere thanks for your support.

Maximo G. Licauco III
President
Filstar Distributors, Corp.
Philippine Licensee of Hallmark Cards, Inc.

Maria Katrina M. Yatco
Business Co-Chair
Leukemic Indigents Fund Endowment
