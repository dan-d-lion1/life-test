About Us

 

REGISTRATION

L.I.F.E. is a non-stock, non-profit foundation organized in February 1995 and registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission of the Philippines on July 17, 1995.  Its registration number is A1095-02652.

PEOPLE

The spirit behind the Foundation is Dr. Alendry P. Caviles, Jr., a compleat pediatrician, internist, and oncologist of 36 years.  His primary practice is  concentrated on Hematology and Immunology.  Dr. Caviles, the past  President of the Philippine Society of Allergology and  Immunology, heads  the Hematology Unit and President of Dr. Fe del Mundo Medical Center Foundation Philippines, Inc. formerly (Children’s Medical Center Philippines and General Hospital, Inc.) and is a Professor Emeritus of the University of the Philippines.

The Board of Trustees is composed of Ramon Ang, Angelita Avena, Rafael  Azanza, George  Canseco, Alendry Caviles, Jr., Manolo Clavio, Victorio Estepa, Gabriel Felix, Winfred Huang, Angelina Panganiban, Yozo Taniguchi, Grace Whang Cruz, Emma Yap and Ma.  Katrina M. Yatco.  The Honorary Trustee is Dr. Fe del Mundo.

OFFICERS

The fficers are:  Alendry Caviles, Jr., Chairman; Winfred Huang, President; Manolo Clavio, Vice-President; Gabriel Felix, Secretary; and Angelina Panganiban, Auditor.

OBJECTIVES

Guided by its vision, L.I.F.E. exists to serve two purposes. The first is to assist leukemic children  from indigent families achieve remission.  The second is to provide optimum care in response to the children’s material, psychosocial, and spiritual needs.

PROGRAMS

There are three programs that the Foundation is currently implementing.

Adoption. The program connects donors and 25 leukemic children yearly on a donor-patient basis for remission treatment.

Support Group Training. L.I.F.E. has established a Support Group to help patients and parents  cope with and manage the disease.  A training program for  prospective counselors has been initiated with the assistance of De La Salle  University’s Counselor Education Department and the St. Luke’s Cancer Institute.

Information. The Foundation is the recipient of a generous offer from the advertising agency JIMENEZ/D’ARCY ADVERTISING formerly     JIMENEZ/DMB&B to develop and implement a print advertising campaign to disseminate information and help raise funds for its operations.

In time and when resources allow L.I.F.E. intends to independently subsidize the  cost of treatment and medication of patients.

FINANCES

L.I.F.E. depends exclusively on donations and fund-raising proceeds.  All     personnel, except for the clerical staff, serve gratis.

OFFICES

The office of L.I.F.E. is located at Dr. Fe del Mundo Medical Center Foundation,Philippines formerly Children’s Medical Center, Philippines and General Hospital, Inc. 11 Banawe Street, Quezon City, Philippines.  Its phone numbers are 63-2-740-7346 and telefax 63-2-743-1818 or you can visit us at www.life.org.ph or e-mail us at lifeinfo@life.org.ph
