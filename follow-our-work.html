What does L.I.F.E. do?

Dubbed as Hallmark for L.I.F.E ., the project is sponsored by Filstar Distributors Corporation. Sports for L.I.F.E . is a mini-Olympics event exclusive to the patients and their families. This serves not only to raise funds but also to provide the patients respite from the rigors of chemotherapy. The event is held every last Sunday of April at the Quezon Memorial Circle.

L.I.F.E Foundation Day Celebrations find the people behind L.I.F.E. coming together to thank God for the healing of those patients cured during the past 12 months, and for the benefactors who lent support to the Foundation the past year.   They are held between July 13, the birthday of L.I.F.E. Founder Dr. Alendry Caviles, Jr.(+), and July 17, the anniversary of the incorporation of the Foundation.

L.I.F.E. Christmas Parties see all patients of L.I.F.E., their families, friends, attending physicians, and volunteers celebrate Christmas in prayer to the Source of Life, and in sharing laughter from games and other fun-filled activities.   These joyous events are held every third Saturday of December.

Over its first 14 years, the Foundation was engaged in a number of activities intended to create awareness of the Foundation and to raise funds:

  • "The Dolphin", "The Forest", "The Eagle" print advertising, this was a campaign developed early on by Jimenez/DMB&B (now Publicis JimenezBasic). This was meant to create awareness of the Foundation and raise even token donations of P20 from each benefactor. The leukemic children themselves formed the dolphin, the forest, and the eagle used for the ads. Poor families continue to send their P20.00 through the mail from time to time.
  • "An Evening for L.I.F.E",  this was a concert organized by the late Filipino composer and L.I.F.E. Trustee George Canseco and his friends, for the benefit of L.I.F.E.
  • A premier showing of "A Time to Kill".
  • Showing of the musical play "Sleeping Beauty" by Repertory Philippines .
  • "Art and Heart", this was an exhibit of Chinese paintings organized for the benefit of L.I.F.E.. "
  • "Padyakan Para sa mga Batang may Leukemia", this was a bike-run participated in by 300 cyclists touring Quezon City, San Juan, Mandaluyong City, Makati City and Manila. Life Cycles Foundation, Inc. sponsored this event.
  • "Bikers for L.I.F.E.",  this was a bloodletting campaign sponsored by the Federation of Motorcycles. L.I.F.E. children were given the thrill of riding motorcycles around Makati.
