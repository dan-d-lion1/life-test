UP Medical Students Society, November 26

  • First day of the proposed activity of the UPMSS (UP Medical Students' Society) to LIFE

A simple program/party with games and activities aimed at getting acquainted with the L.I.F.E. patients.

The UPMSS would like to extend their support to L.I.F.E. by being a long term partner and by holding several activities/events together with LIFE in an aim to provide psychosocial support and resources through promotion and publicity.

They are planning to give lectures/workshops aimed at teaching patients proper hand washing, exercise, nutrition, disease prevention, etc. This would probably be on January 2012.

They also planned to conduct fieldtrip (tentative, Fieldtrip at the NIDO Science Discovery Center at the Mall of Asia) for the patients sometime in March 2012.
