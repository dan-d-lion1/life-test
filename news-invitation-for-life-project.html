An Invitation for L.I.F.E Project

LIFE Admin | Monday, 22 October 2012 04:29
Home / News

Greetings from Filstar Distributors Corporation!

Our company is the exclusive licensee of Hallmark Greeting Cards here in the Philippines. With our aim of helping businesses and inpiduals enrich their lives through our inspiring creations, we have also partnered with the Leukemic Indigents Fund Endowment (L.I.F.E.) Foundation to reach out and support young Filipinos who suffer from the dreaded leukemia disease through our charitable endeavor called Hallmark for L.I.F.E.

Hallmark for L.I.F.E. is an annual project wherein we showcase an array of true-blue Pinoy Christmas cards wholeheartedly donated by talented local artists and photographers. With every set of cards you buy at Php 26.00 per piece , you contribute to the funds needed to continue the medical treatment of poor leukemic children under the wing of L.I.F.E.

On our 17th year, we encourage you to be a part of this cause and help bring hope and a brighter future to our young brothers and sisters. Cherish the people who matter to you and make your cheers count this season as you fulfill a promise of hope to a child whose life deserves second chance through your participation and support.

For inquiries or placement of orders, please feel free to get in touch with us at 636-5051 local 105/113 or 09182928528 / 0927-2271155.

May the joy and peace of the coming holiday season be with you today and always!
